About Me

Hi, I'm Aaliyah! I'm a dedicated human services professional with a passion for helping others and driving positive change in the world. When I'm not busy making a difference, you'll find me embracing the beauty of nature and capturing its breathtaking scenery through my lens.

The inspiration behind creating this website was to blend my love for humanitarian work and scenic photography. Here, I blog about my experiences in humanitarianism and share my favorite scenic photos. I hope you enjoy your stay and find inspiration in my journey!
